Helpful Hints...

Changing the Wireless Network Name (SSID) is the first step in securing your wireless network. Please change it into a name that is familiar to you and does not contain any personal information.

If you enable Auto Channel Scan, the router selects the best possible channel for your wireless network to operate on.

Setting the Visibility Status to Invisibleis another way of securing your network. In the Invisible state, wireless clients cannot detect your wireless network when they use the auto scan function. To connect your wireless devices to the router, manually enter the SSID on each device. Please save the information of your SSID properly.

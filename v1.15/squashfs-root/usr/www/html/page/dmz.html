DMZ

The DSL router forwards IP packets that do not belong to any application configured in the Port Forwarding list, from WAN to the DMZ host.

Enter IP address of the computer and click "Apply" to enable the DMZ host.

Clear the field of the IP address and click "Apply" to disable the DMZ host.

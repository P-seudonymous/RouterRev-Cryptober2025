If IGMP snooping is enabled, the device (L2 switch) can perform intelligent multicast forwarding (only) toward those hosts, such as IPSTBs, which request to join (as members of) a specific multicast group, such as an IPTV channel, within the broadcast domain (same PVC/VLAN). As a result, it significantly reduces traffic flooding upon interfaces that are not registered as receivers of the specific multicast group.
